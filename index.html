What is FESCO?

Before we dive into the duplicate bill process, a quick introduction: FESCO is one of the leading electricity distributors in Pakistan, serving districts like Faisalabad, Sargodha, Mianwali, and many others. With millions of customers, it’s no wonder they’ve made it easy for you to access your bill online if something goes wrong.

Why Would You Need a Duplicate Bill?

We’ve all been there—your bill just disappears. Whether it’s because the mailman took an unexpected detour or you simply misplaced it, you still have to pay it on time to avoid late fees or power disconnections. Here’s where the FESCO duplicate bill service comes in handy.

Here’s How to Get Your FESCO Duplicate Bill

Thankfully, FESCO’s online system is user-friendly (unlike some other government websites we won’t name). Just follow these simple steps:

  • Go to the FESCO Website: Open your favorite web browser and search FESCO Duplicate Bill. Don’t worry—it’s not like one of those spammy sites that make you feel like your computer will crash. This one’s legit.
  • Locate the Duplicate Bill Section: Once you’re on the homepage, look for the “Duplicate Bill” option. Usually, it’s easy to find since they know it’s the most popular thing people visit their site for (because, let’s face it, we all lose our bills).
  • Enter Your Reference Number: To fetch your bill, you’ll need to enter your reference number. This is the long number on your previous bill—usually in the top right corner. If you have no idea where your old bill is, you might need to dig through some papers or ask a neighbor.
  • View and Download Your Bill: Voila! Your duplicate bill will appear on the screen, like magic. You can either view it online or download it to print later.
  • Pay the Bill: Now that you’ve got your bill, don’t forget the most important part—paying it! You can either head to your nearest bank, post office, or use online payment services if you're feeling tech-savvy.

Pro Tips for a Smooth Experience

  • Save Your Reference Number: It’s always a good idea to save your reference number somewhere safe, like your phone or a note where you’ll actually remember it.
  • Keep a Digital Copy: Once you’ve downloaded your duplicate bill, keep a digital copy on your phone or computer.
  • Check Your Bill Regularly: Even if you haven’t lost your bill, checking it online can save you from any surprises.
